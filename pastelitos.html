Pastelitos

Many bakeries in Miami carry Cuban pastelitos and other small bites. These include:


  • pastelitos de guayaba (guava pastries)
  • pastelitos de guayaba y queso (guava and cheese pastries)
  • pastelitos de queso (cheese pastries)
  • pastelitos de carne (meat pastries)
  • croquetas de jamón, pollo, o espinaca (ham, chicken, or spinach croquettes)
